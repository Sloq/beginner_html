Bread Perfected: Description and History

Cooking Your Toast: Proper Temperature Control

  1. Slice bread
  2. Put into toaster
  3. Cook for a maximum of 3 minutes
  4. Spread liberally with butter
  5. Add additional toppings(butter is required first because it needs the proper heat to melt properly)
  6. Enjoy toast with a glass of milk or a mimosa
